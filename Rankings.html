Overall Ratings

Overall rankings for translation groups read so far.

This page transfers the current work-in-progress tier list into a browsable site format. Detailed score sheets are available on the Translation Groups page where finalized breakdowns exist.

Tier 5: Official-Quality

Listed in no particular order.

  1. BoredCoronaKids - Excellent.
  2. Leslie and the Victims - Excellent.

Tier 4: Good, but not official quality

Best to worst.

  1. KS Group - Good quality with decent translations.
  2. LHTranslation - Series-dependent.
  3. Asurascans - Fairly consistent, but can be series-dependent.
  4. Flamecomics - Similar to Asura.

Tier 3: Readable, but not good

Most readable to least readable.

  1. DivaScans - Most readable.
  2. thunderscans - Usually pretty decent, but can be series-dependent.
  3. QI Scans - Fairly consistent.
  4. StoneScape - Consistent.
  5. RinkoComics - Weird grammar and broken context, but readable.
  6. Violet Scans
  7. Valir Scans
  8. Siren Scans
  9. ROKARI COMICS - Similar to Rinko, but more formal than awkward.
  10. SnowyPuffx - Similar to Rinko, with less weird grammar and more context breaking.
  11. EZMANGA - Good enough when there is no other option from tiers 3, 4, or 5.

Tier 2: Improved, declined, or inconsistent

Listed in no particular order.

  • UTOON - Declined recently.
  • Lua Comic - Improved recently; inconsistent overall.
  • RESET SCANS - Usually very good, but can be inconsistent on occasion.
  • XNutScans - Very inconsistent and usually pretty bad, but readable on occasion.
  • Vortexscans - Series-dependent and very hit or miss.
  • GenzToons - Sometimes decent, sometimes terrible.

Tier 1: Avoid

Worst to tolerable.

  1. MagusManga - Low-quality MTL history, though recently improved.
  2. Nyx Scans - Same tier as MagusManga.
  3. Necro Scans - Slightly better than UTOON, still terrible.
  4. Fairy Scans - Slightly above Necro, still bad.
  5. Luna Toons - Inconsistent; occasionally decent, usually unreadable.
  6. Eris Scans - Modern UTOON-tier MTL.
  7. Timeless Toons - Not worth mentioning beyond this.
  8. Eva Scans - Another Modern UTOON-tier group.
  9. Philliascans - Overall terrible, but has some outliers.
